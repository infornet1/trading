Three Strategies, One Truth

Run all three approaches on the same price data and see exactly where fees, impermanent loss, and hedging costs net out.

STRATEGY 01
💵

HODL 50/50

Hold 50% BTC + 50% USDT. No active management. Pure price exposure as the baseline benchmark.

  • Zero gas / protocol costs
  • Full upside on BTC price
  • No yield on idle USDT
  • 50% portfolio in stablecoin
STRATEGY 02
📈

LP Only

Provide concentrated liquidity in a BTC/USDT pool. Earn trading fees while being exposed to impermanent loss when price moves outside your range.

  • Earn LP fees continuously
  • Amplified capital efficiency vs v2
  • Impermanent loss when out-of-range
  • Active range management required
STRATEGY 03
🛡

LP + Hedge

Combine LP fee income with a perpetuals SHORT that activates when price drops below your range — capturing downside protection while keeping fee yield.

  • LP fees + SHORT perp gains
  • IL offset by hedge P&L
  • Configurable hedge ratio
  • Funding rate cost on SHORT

Pipeline

How The Backtest Works

Four deterministic steps from raw price data to a full performance report.

01

Fetch Price Data

Pull OHLCV candles from Binance for your chosen date range and interval (1h / 4h / 1d).

02

Simulate LP Position

Model Uniswap v3 concentrated liquidity math — token balances, fees earned, and in/out-of-range status per candle.

03

Apply Hedge Logic

Open SHORT perp when price falls below lower tick. Apply funding rate costs and PnL on each candle while out-of-range.

04

Report & Compare

Calculate Sharpe, Sortino, Max Drawdown, Profit Factor and plot equity curves side-by-side across all three strategies.

📊
Sortino Ratio
∞
Like Sharpe but only penalises downside deviation — better for asymmetric strategies.
📋
Max Drawdown
%
Largest peak-to-trough equity drop. Key measure of capital risk at the worst moment.
📌
Profit Factor
ratio
Total gains divided by total losses per candle. Values >1.5 indicate a strong edge.
